Capstone Project

Hotel Multi-Restaurant
Ordering System

QR-based in-room dining for hotels. Guests scan, browse, order, and track — all from their phone.

FastAPI PostgreSQL Async SQLAlchemy Vanilla JS Pydantic Jinja2
Why This Project

The Problem & Solution

Traditional Room Service
  • ✗ Phone calls to a busy front desk
  • ✗ Paper menus that go out of date
  • ✗ No visibility into order status
  • ✗ Single restaurant option only
  • ✗ Manual billing, error-prone
Our Digital Solution
  • ✓ Scan QR in room, order instantly
  • ✓ Live digital menus, always current
  • ✓ Real-time status tracking per order
  • ✓ 7 restaurants, one seamless app
  • ✓ Room bill or pay now — guest's choice
